Critical Listening Assistant

Critical Listening Assistant is an AI-assisted learning project currently under private development. It explores how technology can support careful listening, thoughtful reflection, and intellectual engagement across lectures, meetings, discussions, and media without replacing the listener's own judgment.

Overview

Listening as an active intellectual skill.

Critical Listening Assistant is designed around the idea that listening is an active intellectual practice rather than a passive activity. The project explores how AI can help learners organize ideas, reflect on discussions, and engage more carefully with complex material while preserving independent judgment and critical thinking.

This project reflects my broader interest in critical thinking, AI literacy, philosophy of education, and human-centered educational technology.

Focus areas

What it emphasizes

  • Reflection and note organization
  • Media literacy
  • Human-centered AI
