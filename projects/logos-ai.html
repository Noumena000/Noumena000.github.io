Logos AI

Logos AI is an AI chatbot and workspace currently under private development. It explores how privacy-conscious AI can support reasoning, writing, reflection, and knowledge work while keeping users in control of their own thinking.

Status

Private Development
Interface previews and demonstrations will be released as development progresses.

Purpose

To create an AI chatbot that feels legible, calm, and grounded in the user's goals rather than encouraging passive dependence.

Preview Coming Soon This project is currently under active development. Selected interface previews and demonstrations will be published as the project approaches public release.

Overview

A chatbot for clearer AI interaction.

Logos AI is designed as a thoughtful chatbot and workspace for interacting with AI. Rather than encouraging frictionless over-automation, the project emphasizes clarity, privacy, and human agency so users remain intentional and engaged throughout the reasoning process.

The project direction is centered on helping users stay thoughtful, intentional, and in command of AI-supported conversation and work.

Highlights

Focus areas

  • Privacy-conscious AI interaction
  • Human-centered control and clarity
  • Calm workspace design
  • Human judgment at the center
