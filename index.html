About

I am an Associate Professor of Philosophy with interests in ethics, critical thinking, applied philosophy, artificial intelligence, educational design, and the future of learning. This site collects my academic work, teaching materials, and software projects.

Research & Publications

My research interests include applied ethics, Socratic pedagogy, moral reasoning, social epistemology, philosophy of technology, and the role of AI in education and public life.

Research areas

  • Ethics and applied philosophy
  • AI, agency, and human judgment
  • Socratic method and critical thinking
  • Educational technology and learning design

Teaching

My teaching focuses on making philosophy accessible, practical, and intellectually rigorous. I emphasize ethical reasoning, argument analysis, reflective judgment, and student-centered learning.
